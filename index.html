About

Unapologetically
Everything

"I want to be remembered as someone who was unafraid."

Harry Adair is a Texas-based model, actor, musician, and visual artist — not because he couldn't choose, but because he refused to. His work spans the editorial and the gritty, the studio and the overgrown field, the stage and the street.

Classically trained as a clarinetist (All State), he brings the same precision and emotional range to every medium he touches. His modeling work defies easy categorization — fashion-forward, gender-fluid, unapologetically physical. His acting credits include theater and film, with the ambition to match.
